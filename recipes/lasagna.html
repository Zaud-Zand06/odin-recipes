Lasagna

Description

Artisnal, hand-pulled pasta noodles gently rest atop a thoughtful layering of red-sauce and ground meat.

The delicate interplay between falling noodle and cascading sauce,

should invoke the imagery of a bustling spring brook, leading into a gentle waterfall.

Ingredients Needed:

  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasgna Noodle
  • Cheese
  • Egg
