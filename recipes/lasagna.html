Lasagna

Description

Artisinal, hand-pulled pasta noodles gently rest atop a thoughtful layering of red-sauce and ground meat.

The delicate interplay between falling noodle and cascading sauce,

should invoke the imagery of a bustling spring brook, leading into a gentle waterfall.

Ingredients Needed:

  • 1 pound of sweet italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound of lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup of minced onions
  • 2 cloves of garlic, crushed or minced
  • 28 oz can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 6.5 oz cans of tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • white sugar
  • chopped parsley
  • dried basil
  • salt
  • italian seasoning
  • fennel seed
  • black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 oz ricotta
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pound mozarella
  • 3/4 cup parmesan

How to Make:

  1. Make the Meat Sauce

    2. Cook the sausage, ground beef, onion and garlic in a Dutch oven over medium heat until well browned.

    Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water. Add the seasonings and let simmer while covered for around an hour and a half. Stir occasionally.

  2. Cook the Noodles

    3. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cool the lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8-10 minutes. Drain the noodles and rinse with cold water.

  3. Make the Filling

    4. Combine the ricotta cheese with the egg, some parsley, and some salt.

    Now is a great time to pre-heat the oven as well. 190 degrees C or 375 degrees F.

  4. Assembly

    5. Spread a good amount of meat sauce into the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over the meat sauce. Spread with 1/2 of the ricotta filling, and top with 1/3 of the mozarella. Spoon 1/12 cups of meat sauce over the mozarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup Parmesan.

    Repeat this layering and top it with the remaining mozarella and parmesan.

    Next, cover the top with foil. Be careful not to toch the chheese with the foil.

  5. Bake and Rest

    6. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes with the foil. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes.

    Then let the lasagna rest for 15 minutes before serving!
